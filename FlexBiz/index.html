F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for an MVP project?

For most startups, we delivered a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) within 4 to 8 weeks. This rapid cycle allows you to validate your core idea with real users while maintaining high code quality and scalability.

How do you ensure the security of our data?

Security is integrated into every stage of our development lifecycle. We implement AES-256 encryption, follow SOC2/GDPR compliance standards, and conduct regular penetration testing to ensure your enterprise data remains bulletproof.

Can you integrate with our existing legacy systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in building robust API layers and microservices that bridge the gap between modern cloud infrastructure and your existing legacy databases or ERP systems, ensuring a seamless digital transformation.

What is your primary technology stack?

We leverage industry-leading technologies including React for frontend, Spring Boot and Node.js for backend, and AWS/Azure for cloud infrastructure. This stack ensures maximum performance, security, and developer productivity.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive 24/7 post-launch support and maintenance packages. We act as your long-term technical partner, helping you scale features and maintain system health as your business grows.
